# Playbook: Exchange Listing

Process owner (RACI Accountable): **Joint (CEO + CPWO)** — irreversible, cross-domain per Decision Matrix

## When to Use

Pursuing a CEX/DEX listing per the phase-by-phase roadmap in
`docs/WHITEPAPER.md` §14.2.

## Preconditions

- TGE completed (`docs/playbooks/TGE.md`)
- Liquidity pool established and, per `docs/TOKENOMICS.md` §Requirements,
  liquidity locked and website live
- Legal & Compliance has reviewed listing-jurisdiction requirements for the
  target exchange(s)

## Steps

1. **Target selection** — Web3 & Token Strategy proposes exchange tier/target
   (per `docs/WHITEPAPER.md` §14 Listing Strategy) based on TGE market cap and
   listing roadmap phase.
2. **Legal review** — Legal & Compliance checks exchange-specific compliance
   requirements (KYC/AML for the listing entity, jurisdiction restrictions).
3. **Auditor review** — Independent Auditor reviews listing terms (listing fee,
   market-making commitments, lock-up terms) for risk.
4. **Joint go/no-go** — Marina and Vadim approve before signing any listing
   agreement — this is Joint because listing terms and public visibility affect
   the whole company's credibility, not just token mechanics.
5. **Execute** — Web3 & Token Strategy manages the listing process and exchange
   relationship.
6. **Announce** — Growth/Marketing prepares announcement content; any claim
   about platform/medical credibility in listing announcements routes through
   Medical Advisory first.

## Where This Lands

Listing readiness checklist and status: `docs/reports/web3/`. Go/no-go decision:
`docs/decision-log/`.
