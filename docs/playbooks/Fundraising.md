# Playbook: Fundraising

Process owner (RACI Accountable): **CPWO (Vadim)** for instrument/outreach strategy;
**Joint** for terms affecting company-wide liability

## When to Use

Preparing materials for, or executing, a fundraising round (private sale,
strategic partner round, SAFT, equity).

## Steps

1. **Materials** — Web3 & Token Strategy prepares the pitch deck, cap table
   impact analysis, and investor tracker, drawing on `docs/TOKENOMICS.md` and
   `docs/WHITEPAPER.md`.
2. **Legal review** — Legal & Compliance reviews the SAFT/token warrant
   template and instrument choice for regulatory soundness before any investor
   sees it.
3. **CEO consult** — Marina is consulted before terms are finalized, since
   investor terms create company-wide liability (RACI Fundraising row) even
   though instrument/outreach strategy itself is CPWO-only.
4. **Auditor review** — Independent Auditor reviews cap table impact and terms
   for risk (dilution, control provisions, disclosure gaps).
5. **Outreach** — Web3 & Token Strategy executes outreach per the approved
   strategy.
6. **Term sheet → Joint sign-off** — final terms require both founders' sign-off
   before signing, per Decision Matrix ("equity or token allocation" and
   "company-wide liability" both apply here).
7. **Close** — Legal & Compliance finalizes documentation.

## Where This Lands

Deck, cap table analysis, investor tracker, and round status:
`docs/reports/fundraising/`. Final terms sign-off: `docs/decision-log/`.
