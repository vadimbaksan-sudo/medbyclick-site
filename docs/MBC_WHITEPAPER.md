### 4.2 Founder Locked Allocation (25%)

**25,000,000 MBC** — Personal tokens of the two co-founders (12,500,000 each).

**Vesting Schedule:**
- **Cliff:** 12 months (no tokens released)
- **Linear unlock:** 48 months after cliff
- **Total vesting period:** 60 months (5 years)

This signals to exchanges, investors, and partners: *"We are here to build a company, not to sell tokens."*

**Why the founder split into two categories?**

Separating the founders' personal allocation (25%) from the Innovation Fund (10%) prevents a common governance problem: when founders need tokens for strategic moves (hiring a CTO, acquiring technology, closing a partnership), it should NOT look like they're "giving away their personal share." A dedicated fund makes the structure more flexible and professional.

### 4.3 Founder Innovation Fund (10%)

**10,000,000 MBC** — Under company control, used exclusively for:
- Hiring key personnel (CTO, medical advisors)
- Technology acquisitions
- Strategic deals and partnerships
- Critical integrations

This fund is NOT personal tokens. It exists to solve unpredictable future challenges without diluting the founders' personal stake.
