## Job Description — Patient Coordinator, MedByClick

**Location:** Israel (on-site/hybrid) · **Reports to:** Marina (CEO) · **Start:** Month 2 target

### About the role

MedByClick connects Russian-speaking patients in Israel to vetted specialist
doctors through a trust network built over 40 years of community relationships.
The Coordinator is the first hire who will operate inside that trust network
directly — not just booking appointments, but making the same kind of routing
judgment the founder currently makes personally: which case goes to which
doctor, and which cases need to be flagged rather than routed automatically.
This is a clinical triage role, not an administrative scheduling role.

### What you'll do

- Intake new patient cases: gather relevant history, symptoms, and urgency
  signals from patients in Russian and/or Hebrew
- Route cases to the appropriate vetted doctor based on the triage criteria
  and vetting standard set by Medical Advisory
- Flag cases that fall outside established routing patterns, involve doctor
  hesitation, or carry any adverse-outcome or complaint signal — per the
  quality-review triage protocol
- Maintain doctor and patient communication through the case lifecycle
- Escalate anything with a clinical-safety dimension immediately, rather than
  resolving it independently

### Required qualifications

- **Medical coordination background** — prior experience in patient intake,
  case management, or clinical coordination (hospital, clinic, medical
  tourism, or insurance case-management setting)
- **Russian and Hebrew, both fluent** — spoken and written; this is
  non-negotiable given the patient population and doctor network
- **Demonstrated intake/triage experience** — has actually made or supported
  routing decisions based on incoming case information, not only scheduled
  appointments off a fixed protocol
- Comfortable working with health information under confidentiality
  obligations

### Strong preference (not required)

- Existing familiarity with the Israeli healthcare system and specialist
  referral norms
- Prior experience in a role reporting directly to a founder/small team,
  where judgment calls without a large support structure are routine

### Explicitly not this role

- Pure scheduling/calendar management with no triage component
- No language requirement flexibility — English-only or Hebrew-only
  candidates do not meet the bar regardless of other qualifications
