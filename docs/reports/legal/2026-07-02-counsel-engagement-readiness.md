## Summary

`docs/LEGAL_BRIEF.md` has been substantively ready to send to counsel since v1.0
— the token model, tokenomics, entity options, and open legal questions are all
documented. What was missing was not legal analysis but engagement mechanics: no
firm has been contacted, no NDA exists, no engagement signatory or retainer
funding source has been decided, and founder identity fields are still
placeholders. None of this is a legal *risk* finding in the usual sense — it's a
process gap that has kept the brief from ever leaving draft status.

## What changed

Added Part 0 to `docs/LEGAL_BRIEF.md`: a blocking-gap table, an explicit list of
what is *not* blocking (so it doesn't get conflated with the real open legal
questions in Parts 2–8), an outreach tracker table to populate as contact is
made, and a recommended sequencing.

## Decisions required (routing per `docs/governance/DECISION_MATRIX.md`)

These are **Joint** — retainer/engagement spend is a financial-liability decision
for the company as a whole, not a Legal & Compliance call:

1. Which firm(s) to approach first (candidates already listed in the brief:
   Fieldfisher, DLx Law, MME Legal, Hogan Lovells, Sygna Partners).
2. Who signs the engagement letter pre-incorporation (no entity exists yet).
3. How the retainer is funded and split, given no treasury/token proceeds exist
   yet — necessarily personal founder funds at this stage.

One item is **not** a decision, just an action: founders supplying their own
identity/KYC details directly to counsel once an NDA is in place (gap #5 in the
brief) — this is not something Legal & Compliance can source.

## Escalation status

Not blocking TGE, listing, or a whitepaper publish on its own, so this does not
trigger the immediate-escalation rule — filed via normal routing. Flagging now
because the Appendix A target of "Legal entity incorporated — August 2026"
depends on a jurisdiction recommendation from counsel, which depends on this
engagement happening first; the runway is short if outreach doesn't start soon.

## Next step

Awaiting founder decision on the three Joint items above before outreach can
