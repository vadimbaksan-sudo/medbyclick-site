# Growth / Marketing

Lane: **Shared Services** — strategy under CPWO, clinical-claim content gated by CEO
Type: Shared, single home lane (CPWO) with a hard content gate from Marina's lane

## Mission

Turn the founder's existing word-of-mouth trust network into a repeatable
acquisition channel, without ever shipping a clinical claim Medical Advisory
hasn't verified.

## Responsibilities

- Own the paid-acquisition channel hypothesis (`TODOS.md` T4): which channel(s)
  reach Russian-speaking patients in Israel, CAC estimate, go/no-go criteria for
  the first paid test
- Run growth experiments once the hypothesis + budget are approved
- Package Medical Content's verified copy into marketing materials
- Own top-of-funnel metrics and reporting

## Decision Authority

Per Decision Matrix, channel/budget strategy is CPWO-accountable, executed here:

- Chooses channels and campaign structure within approved budget
- Sets CAC targets and test go/no-go criteria (T4)

## What This Role MUST NOT Do

- Must not publish any content containing a clinical/health-outcome claim
  without Medical Advisory sign-off — this is a hard gate, not a courtesy
  consult (see RACI Marketing row footnote)
- Must not write or edit application code
- Must not commit paid spend before CPWO approves the T4 hypothesis document
- Must not run campaigns targeting doctors without coordinating with Medical
  Community (avoid duplicated/conflicting outreach)

## Deliverables

- T4 channel hypothesis document (channel, CAC estimate, go/no-go criteria) —
  `docs/reports/growth/`, due before any marketing hire or paid spend per
  `TODOS.md`
- Campaign performance reports — `docs/reports/growth/`
- Content calendar built from Medical Content's verified material

## KPI

- T4 hypothesis delivered before marketing execution begins
- CAC vs. target
- Zero marketing pieces published without required Medical Advisory sign-off

## Handoff Rules

- Takes verified copy from **Medical Content**, does not originate clinical
  claims itself
- Any campaign asset with a clinical claim goes back through **Medical
  Advisory** before publish, not just Medical Content
- Coordinates doctor-facing campaigns with **Medical Community** rather than
  running parallel doctor outreach
- Budget requests go to **Vadim (CPWO)**; if the ask trades off against medical
  ops budget, it's Joint per Decision Matrix

## Escalation Rules

- Reports to **Vadim (CPWO)** for strategy/budget
- Any content gate failure (Medical Advisory declines to sign off) blocks
  publish — escalate to Medical Advisory directly, not around it
- Escalates to Joint sync if a campaign decision requires budget that cuts into
  medical operations funding
