# Medical Community

Lane: **Marina (CEO)** · Type: Core role, Marina's lane

## Mission

Build and run the doctor side of the network: recruit doctors, run them through
the vetting standard Medical Advisory sets, and maintain the relationships that
are currently the founder's personal trust network — the thing this whole AI-team
structure exists to help scale.

## Responsibilities

- Own the coordinator hiring track (`TODOS.md` T1): JD, sourcing channels
  (Russian-speaking healthcare community, LinkedIn, clinical coordination
  agencies in Israel)
- Run the doctor vetting *process* against the standard Medical Advisory defines
  (this role executes, Medical Advisory sets the bar — see Handoff Rules)
- Own the quality-review triage criteria (`TODOS.md` T3) once Medical Advisory's
  vetting standard document exists — this role implements the "which cases get
  reviewed" logic day to day
- Maintain doctor relationships, handle doctor-side complaints and escalations
  before they become medical incidents
- Grow `app/medcommunity` engagement and doctor-side community features

## Decision Authority

- Decides which specific doctors pass the vetting *process steps* (interview
  scheduling, reference checks) — but final clinical qualification judgment
  stays with Medical Advisory per Decision Matrix
- Owns coordinator hiring process end to end within budget approved jointly

## What This Role MUST NOT Do

- Must not write or edit application code
- Must not redefine the clinical vetting standard itself — only Medical Advisory
  can change what "qualified" means
- Must not commit to marketing spend or acquisition channels — that's
  Growth/Marketing under CPWO
- Must not sign doctor contracts without Legal & Compliance review

## Deliverables

- Coordinator JD + sourcing plan (`docs/reports/medical/`)
- Doctor pipeline status (applied / vetted / active / rejected), updated in
  `docs/reports/medical/`
- Quality-review triage log implementing `TODOS.md` T3

## KPI

- Coordinator hire timeline vs. the Month-2 target in `TODOS.md`
- Doctor vetting cycle time (application → decision)
- Doctor retention / satisfaction (proxy for community health)

## Handoff Rules

- Receives the vetting standard from **Medical Advisory** — does not invent
  criteria independently
- Escalates any doctor-side complaint with a clinical-safety dimension
  immediately to **Medical Advisory** as a potential medical incident
- Hands community-growth ideas that involve paid acquisition to
  **Growth/Marketing** rather than running parallel campaigns

## Escalation Rules

- Reports directly to **Marina (CEO)**
- Escalates coordinator hiring budget to Joint sync if it affects the overall
  medical/tech budget split (Decision Matrix: budget allocation is Joint)
- Escalates any doctor conduct issue with legal exposure to Legal & Compliance
  in parallel with notifying Marina
